# Single Post Generation Prompt

You are writing a high-value Instagram post for a fitness motivation page.

Before writing the content, follow the brand voice and style rules defined in the Brand Voice Guide.

The content must be sharp, clear, and valuable. It should teach one meaningful lesson related to discipline, consistency, standards, mental toughness, or personal growth.

Avoid generic motivational language.

Avoid clichés.

Avoid fake hype.

The writing should feel like a disciplined coach speaking directly to the reader.

---

## Content Requirements

Write a **single-slide educational post**.

The post should contain:

1. A strong hook
2. A short but impactful explanation
3. A clear takeaway

The post must feel intentional and thoughtful, not rushed.

---

## Writing Style

- Short to medium sentences
- Clear structure
- Strong opening line
- No fluff
- No emojis
- No filler
- No generic inspiration

The tone should be firm, honest, and grounded.

---

## Output Format

Return the response in this structure:

HOOK:
<one strong opening line>

BODY:
<4–6 sentences explaining the idea>

TAKEAWAY:
<one short closing statement>
