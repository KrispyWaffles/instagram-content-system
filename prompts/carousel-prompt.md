# Carousel Content Generation Prompt

You are writing a high-value Instagram carousel for a fitness motivation page.

Before generating content, follow the tone and style defined in the Brand Voice Guide.

The carousel must be educational, structured, and valuable. The goal is to teach something clearly while challenging the reader to think differently about discipline, effort, and self-respect.

Avoid generic motivation.

Avoid clichés.

Avoid fake hype.

Each slide must move the idea forward.

---

## Carousel Requirements

The carousel will contain between **2 and 9 slides**.

Slides should progress logically from one idea to the next.

The structure should follow one of these formats:

- Problem → Cause → Solution
- Mistake → Reality → Correction
- Mindset Shift
- Step-by-Step Framework
- Hard Truth Breakdown

The content should feel sharp, disciplined, and practical.

---

## Writing Style

- Short sentences
- Clear structure
- No fluff
- No emojis
- No filler language
- Avoid generic internet advice
- Keep language simple and powerful

Each slide should contain **1 main idea**.

---

## Slide Structure

Return the output in this format.

SLIDE 1 – HOOK  
<Strong opening statement that stops the scroll>

SLIDE 2  
<Expand on the idea>

SLIDE 3  
<Introduce the lesson>

SLIDE 4  
<Explain the problem or mistake>

SLIDE 5  
<Explain the correction or mindset shift>

SLIDE 6  
<Actionable insight>

SLIDE 7  
<Optional deeper insight>

SLIDE 8  
<Optional supporting point>

SLIDE 9 – CLOSE  
<Summary or challenge to the reader>

---

## Important Rules

- The hook must be strong
- Do not repeat ideas
- Do not sound generic
- Do not write filler content
- Each slide must provide value
- The reader should feel challenged and educated
